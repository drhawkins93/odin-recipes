Roasted Broccoli

Description

Why would you ever steam a vegetable?! Don't steam vegetables!! Roast them! Haven't you ever heard of the Mayard reaction? It makes vegetables delicious!

Ingredients

  • Broccoli florets
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Put broccoli florets in a bowl. Toss with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Lay out on a cookie sheet and bake. Remove from oven once tips of florets have blackened.
  4. Toss in a bit of fresh-squeezed lemon. Enjoy!
